What is DopaBrain?

DopaBrain is a free online platform offering a growing collection of browser-based games, personality tests, and utility tools. Our mission is to provide instant, accessible entertainment and useful tools to everyone — no downloads, no sign-ups, no fees.

What We Offer

  • Games (20+) — Casual and arcade games playable directly in your browser, including classics like 2048, Tetris, Minesweeper, and original titles like Road Shooter
  • Personality Tests (18+) — Fun viral tests including MBTI compatibility, color personality, and brain type quizzes
  • Utility Tools (12+) — Practical tools like QR generators, color palette creators, and typing speed testers
  • Blog (400+ articles) — In-depth articles about games, psychology, and tools in 12 languages

Our Principles

  • Free Forever — All apps and tools are completely free to use
  • No Account Required — Start using any app instantly without sign-up
  • Privacy First — All data stays on your device. We don't collect personal information
  • Multilingual — All apps support 12 languages: Korean, English, Japanese, Chinese, Spanish, Portuguese, French, German, Hindi, Russian, Indonesian, and Turkish
  • Accessible — Keyboard navigation, screen reader support, and reduced motion preferences

Technology

DopaBrain is built with modern web technologies and runs entirely in your browser. Games use HTML5 Canvas and Three.js for 3D rendering. The site is hosted on GitHub Pages with Cloudflare CDN for fast global access.

Supported Languages

All applications are available in: Korean (한국어), English, Japanese (日本語), Chinese (中文), Spanish (Español), Portuguese (Português), French (Français), German (Deutsch), Hindi (हिन्दी), Russian (Русский), Indonesian (Bahasa), Turkish (Türkçe).
