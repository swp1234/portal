What Is the Red Flag Test?

The Red Flag Test is an interactive quiz that analyzes your dating behavior across key dimensions — texting habits, emotional intensity, commitment patterns, boundaries, and communication style — and matches you to the red flag type that best describes your relationship blind spot.

Unlike tests that simply judge you, this quiz is designed with compassion and humor. Everyone has red flags. The goal is not to shame yourself but to recognize patterns that might be sabotaging your connections. Self-awareness is the first step to healthier relationships.

The test uses a multi-dimensional scoring system. Every answer contributes points across all 6 red flag types, producing a nuanced result that reflects your dominant pattern rather than a simple label.

How the Test Works

The Red Flag Test presents you with 8 carefully designed scenarios that explore different facets of your dating personality:

  • Texting behavior — How do you handle digital communication and response times?
  • Social media habits — Do you research potential partners online?
  • Emotional expression — Are your feelings steady or a rollercoaster?
  • Commitment style — Do you follow through on plans or keep your options open?
  • Pace of relationships — Do you take it slow or dive in headfirst?
  • Conflict response — How do you handle disagreements and tension?
  • Boundaries — Are you comfortable with space or do you need constant contact?
  • Decision-making — Are you spontaneous or overthink every choice?

Each answer distributes points across all 6 red flag types using a weighted scoring matrix. After all 8 questions, the type with the highest cumulative score becomes your match. You also receive personalized insights about your dating patterns and actionable tips for growth.

How long does the Red Flag Test take?

The Red Flag Test is designed as a self-awareness tool based on common relationship psychology patterns.
