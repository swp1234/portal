FAQ

Are these tools really free?
Yes, all tools on DopaBrain are 100% free. No hidden charges, no premium tiers, no subscriptions. Every calculator, timer, and generator is free to use without limits.
Do you store my data?
No. All calculations and data processing happen locally in your browser. We do not send, store, or track any of your personal data or inputs.
Can I use these on mobile?
Absolutely! All tools are fully responsive and work perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops. Touch-friendly interfaces ensure a great mobile experience.
Are calculations accurate?
Yes. Our calculators use standard formulas and are thoroughly tested for accuracy. However, for critical financial or medical decisions, always consult a professional.
Do I need to create an account?
No account needed. Just open any tool and start using it immediately. No signup, no login, no email required.
