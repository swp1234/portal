Understanding Zodiac Compatibility

Zodiac compatibility is the study of how different astrological signs interact with each other. Beyond entertainment, it offers a personality framework for understanding relationship dynamics. When two people's birth charts align in specific ways, it can indicate natural harmony or productive tension.

The Four Elements

All 12 zodiac signs belong to one of four classical elements:

  • Fire Signs (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius): Passionate, energetic, action-oriented
  • Earth Signs (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn): Practical, grounded, responsible
  • Air Signs (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius): Intellectual, communicative, social
  • Water Signs (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces): Emotional, intuitive, deep
