FAQ

What is the most compatible MBTI pair?
INFJ and ENFP are widely considered the most compatible. Their shared intuition creates deep understanding, while complementary introversion/extraversion balance energizes the relationship.
Can incompatible MBTI types have good relationships?
Absolutely. MBTI compatibility is a guide, not a rule. Any two types can build a strong relationship with mutual respect, communication, and willingness to understand differences.
Which MBTI types are best for friendship?
Types sharing middle functions bond well. ENFP-INFP, ENTJ-INTJ, and ESFJ-ISFJ pairs often form strong friendships due to shared values and communication styles.
How does MBTI compatibility work?
It's based on cognitive function theory. Types with complementary functions - where one's strength covers the other's weakness - tend to have natural chemistry and balanced relationships.
Is MBTI compatibility scientifically accurate?
MBTI provides useful frameworks for understanding relationship dynamics, but individual maturity, values, and communication skills matter more than type alone.
